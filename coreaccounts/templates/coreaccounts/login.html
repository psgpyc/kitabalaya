Kitabalaya's Reading Marathon

Amidst, the COVID-19 Peril, Kitabalaya has designed a reading marathon that will narrow our boundless energy in a fun and constructive way via the love of books. The marathon is a friendly competition among the bibliophiles that aims to motivate the participants to read a particular book in a specified amount of time. We are aiming to elevate reading culture as a “Must Do” item on the days of wearisome lockdown.
It’s fun and it’s meaningful.
